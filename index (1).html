Our Story

More Than a Salon —
A Place of Empowerment

Beauty Rush Nails Institute is a proudly women-owned beauty brand based in Idimu, Lagos. We combine luxury nail care with world-class professional training, creating a space where beauty meets ambition.

From flawless manicures to advanced nail artistry, our skilled technicians are dedicated to delivering premium results every visit. But we don't stop there — our training institute is shaping the next generation of nail professionals across Nigeria.
